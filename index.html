How to play

  • Cover the board with numbered tetrominoes.
  • Each piece uses exactly four tiles.
  • Tiles must touch along their edges.
  • Read each piece left-to-right, top-to-bottom.
  • The digits in a piece must be non-decreasing and use only 1-4.
  • Tap a completed piece to inspect it, then tap its corner to remove it.
